# Code of Conduct

This project is collaborative engineering work. We expect participants to keep
the space respectful, practical, and safe for other contributors.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions.
- Focus feedback on the code, behavior, data, or process, not on people.
- Share context honestly, including uncertainty, limitations, and mistakes.
- Respect repo boundaries around private extensions, credentials, and
  unpublished data.
- Assume good intent first, then ask for clarification before escalating.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, threats, sexualized language, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing, intimidation, dogpiling, or publishing private information.
- Repeatedly pushing for access to private code, private data, or credentials.
- Knowingly posting secrets, exploit details, or sensitive user material in
  public channels.
- Disruptive behavior that makes collaboration materially harder for others.

## Moderation

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates these
standards. They may also pause or end interactions that are repeatedly
disruptive or unsafe.

## Scope

This policy applies to this repository and to project-managed collaboration
spaces tied to it, including issues, pull requests, code review, and release
coordination.
